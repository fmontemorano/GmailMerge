GmailMerge

  • What is it?

    • A macro for Gmail written in Google Apps Script.

  • What is it for?

    • Sending mass mail merges.

  • How does it work?

    • The macro creates and sends emails based on the information you specify;

      • Name of recipient

      • Address of recipient

      • Attachment

      • Text Body

  • How do I use it?

    • Before starting make sure that you go to Google Contacts (https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/?tab=mm#contacts)  and create the group to which you are planning on sending your mail merge.

    • Open Google Apps Script (https:script.google.com/macros) and create a "blank project".

    • Copy and paste the code, then hit save (name it whatever you want).

    • Run "CreateSpreadsheet".

    • It will ask you to authorize, after you authorize:

      • Re-run “CreateSpreadsheet” then go to Gdrive and open your spreadsheet.

      • Fill out cell H1 of your spreadsheet with the name of the group that will receive your mail merge.

      • Run “LoadContacts”.

      • Once your finished, run “SendMessages” (note: there are no safety features on this prototype macro).
